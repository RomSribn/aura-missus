# Investigations

Any kind of research — technical, product, market, integration, architecture.
Deep dives that inform decisions and features but are not themselves actionable
specs.

An investigation may lead to a decision (`PD-*`), a feature (`PF-*`), or nothing
— just knowledge captured.

**Investigations are history.** Once written, they are not updated — they
capture what was known at the time. If new research is needed, create a new
investigation.
